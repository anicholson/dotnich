My Professional Vision

“People don’t buy what you do; people buy why you do it.”

Here are some aphorisms/phrases that serve as my professional vision; I plan to expand on each one. If it’s not my idea, or if I can’t say it better, I’ll just link to the source. Otherwise, I’ll write up a post.

  • Serve Those Who Serve Others
  • Keep Up With Technological Change
  • Don’t Follow Tech Just Because it’s “the New Hotness”
  • A Good Product/Project Can Defend Its Existence
  • Don’t Build a Platform Until You Know What The Product Is
